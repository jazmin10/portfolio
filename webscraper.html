WEB SCRAPER

SOCCERwebscrapper is a Mongoose-application that collects the top soccer stories from ESPN FC.

CONCEPT

Soccer Webscraper was an opportunity for me to fuse my passion for soccer with my web development skills. For soccer fans, watching a 90-minute game is only part of the story. Fans want to stay up to date with the latest soccer news: scores, postgame news conferences, transfer talk rumors, injury reports, and more.

In order to do so, the application scrapes the Top Stories section from ESPN FC, ESPN's soccer news outlet.

For details, please view Soccer Webscraper's GitHub repository.
