ARTICLE SCRUBBER

NYT Article Scrubber is a MERN application that allows users to search and save articles from the New York Times.

CONCEPT

NYT Article Scrubber's is a Single Page Application using Mongoose, Express, React, and Node.js. This application is a perfect way to search for New York Times articles since September 18, 1851 to today. After a search, users can also save articles of their interest to read later.

For details, please view NYT Article Scrubber's GitHub repository.
