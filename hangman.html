HANGMAN

A replica of the popular game Hangman using Superheroe names for guess words.

CONCEPT

Due to the recent rise of the Marvel and DC cinematic universe, it's difficult not to get caught up in the world of superheroes. Therefore, I decided to fashion one of my first JavaScript projects after the popular comic books. Feel free to play this classic word game - superheroes edition!

For details, please view Hangman's GitHub Repository.
